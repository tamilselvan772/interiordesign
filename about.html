Our interior design mission and vision statement

We create interior design solutions with a perfect combination of style, innovative concepts and functionality's driving force is our relentless passion for emphasizing individuality, which we call intelligent design.

Based in Poole, in the heart of the magnetic Dorset, we draw inspiring ideas from the vast ocean When we create interior design, we invest all our time and love, providing something lasting. In our portfolio you will find selected projects from different areas, both for residential and public spaces.

Each of our projects is unique, created for the right place, bringing emphasized elegance style. As part of our interior design mission and vision, we create inspiring concepts that symbolize elegance and high quality. Our projects include Traditional, Modern, Contemporary, Industrial, as well as many other styles of houses, apartments, offices, restaurants, spas, etc.

What is a design mission?

We call our Interior design mission and vision – Art. The artistic solution and the unique furniture of Urvission Interiors are not just ideas, they are art that creates non-standard spaces in your homes are artistic,non-standard home and cosy atmosphere.

We combine styles, emotions and individuality into one perfect and harmonious project called – Your Project! We present you 10 main styles like: Modern, Contemporary, Minimalist, Industrial, Mid-century Modern and Scandinavian, as part of our interior design mission and vision. But that’s not all! We develop all kinds of projects with an individual approach, non-standard solutions and uniqueness.

We offer not just interior and furniture services, but boutique concepts that will contribute to the home atmosphere, the good moments and last but not least the great comfort they represent for you.
